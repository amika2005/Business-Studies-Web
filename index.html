About Ashoka Kularatne

Dedicated Business Studies Teacher

With over 20 years of teaching experience, Mrs Ashoka Kularatne has helped hundreds of students achieve excellence in their business studies examinations and careers.

Holding a Bachelor's degree in Bsc. Management (HRM), Ashoka combines theoretical knowledge with practical insights to make learning engaging and effective.
